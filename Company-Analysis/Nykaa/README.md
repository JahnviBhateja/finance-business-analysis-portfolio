# Nykaa: Can Profitable Growth Continue?

*A Financial & Strategic Analysis of FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd.*

> **Status:** In Progress

## Objective

This project analyzes Nykaa's business model, financial performance, competitive position, and long-term growth prospects using the FY2024–25 Integrated Annual Report and other publicly available information.

## Key Questions

- How does Nykaa make money?
- What drives its growth?
- Is its profitability sustainable?
- What are the major risks?
- What opportunities lie ahead?
